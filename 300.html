The KTM 300 EXC is a 2-stroke enduro off-road motorcycle made by KTM. The models are the European road legal 300 EXC, the US 300 XC (close-ratio gearbox) and the US 300 XC-W (wide-ratio gearbox). The European EXC versions have a small headlight, speedometer, tail-light and somewhat softer linkless (PDS) rear suspension.
