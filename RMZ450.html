The Suzuki RM-Z450 is a motocross off-road motorcycle made by Suzuki. It was launched in 2004 and has been continuously updated throughout the years in response to new products from Honda, Yamaha, and Kawasaki. The RM-Z450 has been a popular choice among MX riders and has seen success in professional racing events.
